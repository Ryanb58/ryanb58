On-prems - The Engineer's Perspective

Posted on Wed 20 July 2016 in How To

So you want to build an on-prem. Great! On-prems are cash rich enviroments that allow for sales into the entrerprise marketspace without much effort. Right?

Well, from your engineers perspectives, no. Sadly on-prems add a lot of complexity that originally was avoided because of the "cloud". Let's dive into a few of these complexities in which your engineers will face.
